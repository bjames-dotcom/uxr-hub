Project onboarding

Share this with any designer or PM to kick off a research project — replaces the Confluence link

1
Submit a research request
The PM or designer fills out a one-page brief capturing the decision they're trying to make, what they already know, and their timeline. This replaces the vague Slack message.
Fill out request form ↗
2
Research intake + method selection
Align on research questions and pick the right method. Moderated? Survey? In-person? It depends on the decision you're making, not the timeline preference.
Choose method ↗
3
Brief + screener review
Brief is drafted and reviewed before anything goes to field. The collaborator confirms the screener matches who they actually need — not who they assume they need.
Generate brief + screener ↗
4
Fieldwork — collaborator involvement
Designers observe sessions, not moderate. PMs get a live Dovetail link to follow along. No surprises at the readout. Everyone sees what you see, as it happens.
5
Synthesis + insight delivery
Delivered as a topline readout: 3–5 prioritized insights, each with a product implication and a clear ask. Not a 40-slide deck — a decision-ready summary.
Build readout ↗
