Project onboarding

Share this with any designer or PM — replaces the Confluence link copy-paste

1
Submit a research request
Fill out a one-page brief capturing the decision you're trying to make, what you already know, and your timeline. This is not optional — it's how research gets scoped correctly from the start.
Fill out request form with AI ↗
2
Research intake + method selection
Brie will review your request and align on research questions and method. Moderated interviews, a survey, or an in-person visit — the choice depends on the decision you're making, not your timeline preference.
3
Brief + screener review
You review the screener before anything goes to field. This is your chance to confirm we're talking to the right people — not who we assume we need, but who will actually answer the question.
4
Fieldwork — your involvement
Designers observe sessions — they do not moderate. PMs get a live Dovetail link. No surprises at the readout. If you want to know what we're finding, you're welcome in the room.
5
Insight delivery
Findings are delivered as a topline readout: 3–5 prioritized insights, each with evidence, a product implication, and a clear ask addressed to the right person. Not a 40-slide deck.
